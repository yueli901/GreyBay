Careers

Date: 2023-04-13 09:33:20Source: GreyBay Institute [Font: LargeMediumSmall] Background:

At GreyBay Institute we believe that the best research comes from genuine concern for real-world problems, and that the furthest reach begins with a team of idealists working together with professionalism. We are looking for people with scientific rigour, care for others, and a willingness to act—whether you are a researcher or an engineer, if you care about the intersection of cities and health, this is a place where your work can have real impact.

We offer competitive pay, benefits, an open team culture, and above all a stage where your research is seen and used. Here, every line of code, every report, every analysis can help shape policies and tools that improve the health of millions.

2. What we offer

— Collaboration with leading universities, research institutions, and government think tanks so your research addresses real policy agendas;
— Competitive compensation and full social insurance;
— Flexible working, learning fund, annual health check, and paid leave;
— Open, flat team culture where every voice is respected.

3. How to apply

At GreyBay Institute we do not define each other as “employees”—we are research partners and co-creators. We care less about credentials than about solid skills, genuine curiosity, and the drive to turn science into action.

If you share our values and want to grow in a place where your work is seen and respected, please send your CV and portfolio (if any) to hr@greybay.org. Use the subject line “Position name + Your name”. We will respond to shortlisted applicants within ten working days.

We look forward to building healthier cities with you.
